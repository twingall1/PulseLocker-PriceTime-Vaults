About This App

• This site lets you create immutable vaults for PLS, pDAI, or HEX.
• Your tokens are locked until the market price crosses your threshold, or a backup time unlock is reached.
• Price feed uses PulseX pools for the chosen asset (PLS/USDC & DAI backup, pDAI/DAI, HEX/USDC & DAI backup).
• Feeds are chosen by USD-normalized liquidity (using known decimals for DAI=18, USDC=6).
• Vault logic is enforced fully on-chain; no one (including the deployer) can change the rules.

• To deposit: send the chosen token (or PLS) directly to the vault address.
• To withdraw: call Withdraw once the price OR the backup time condition is met.
• Time-based unlock still works even if price pairs are illiquid.
⚠ A 0.5% fee is charged on successful withdrawal.
